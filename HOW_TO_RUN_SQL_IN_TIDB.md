## Method 1: Using TiDB Cloud Web Console (Easiest)

### Step 1: Login to TiDB Cloud
1. Open your browser
2. Go to: **https://tidbcloud.com**
3. Click **"Sign In"** button (top right)
4. Enter your email and password
5. Click **"Sign In"**

### Step 2: Find Your Cluster
1. After login, you'll see the **Dashboard**
2. Look for your cluster named something like:
   - "Cluster-XXXX" or
   - Your custom cluster name
3. The cluster should show:
   - **Status**: Available (green)
   - **Region**: eu-central-1
   - **Host**: gateway01.eu-centra
